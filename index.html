FormulaFPV London 2016

August 20-21, 2016

Race event:

This is the second year for the massive FormulaFPV London event, held once again at the Cheshunt Country Club ground in Cheshunt, North London. Check out their website and Facebook page here and here.

Format:

72 pilots competed in 12 heats of 6 (ten PRO class and two SPORT class).
Four rounds of qualifying, with overall qualifying positions calculated from each competitor's best three scores using the round-by-round (RBR) scoring scheme1.
The top 24 PRO pilots went through to the knockout bracket2* and qualifying positions determined the finals for everybody else.
All races lasted 2 minutes.
For qualifying rounds, race distance was unlimited.
For knockout finals, race distance was set to 4 laps.
For all other finals, race distance was set to 3 laps.

1Round-by-round scoring awards points to each competitor based on their ranking in each round, which is calculated from the number of laps completed and the time taken.
